The Nintendo 3DS

By Toby Brown

The 3DS is a console released by Nintendo in 2011. Over my "many" years of living I have gained much wisdom about said console, and I have played several games.
When I was a wee chap I vividly remember my mum buying me my own Nintendo 2DS- and ever since I was hooked. Ive had the pleasure to play through several games- some good and some bad -
and in doing so the 3DS has become a huge part of who I am, the games supporting me through some big changes in my life.
On this website, I will showcase the history of the console as well some of my favourite games so that you can give em a play as well.
